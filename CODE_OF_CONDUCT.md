# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone. Be respectful, assume good intent, discuss technical decisions with evidence, and accept responsibility for mistakes.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats, publishing private information, or sustained disruption. Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to `conduct@cool-ui.dev`. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.
